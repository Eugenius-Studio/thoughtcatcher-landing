What Gets Deleted

When you delete your Blurts account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Account Information: Your email, name, and profile settings
  • Captured Thoughts: All voice recordings (already auto-deleted after transcription) and transcribed text
  • Integration Connections: Links to Notion, Todoist, and Google Tasks will be disconnected
  • Usage History: All activity logs and analytics data
  • Subscription: Your active subscription will be cancelled (no refunds for remaining time)

What Happens to Your Subscription

If you have an active Pro or Founder Deal subscription:

  • Your subscription will be immediately cancelled
  • You will not be charged again
  • No refunds will be issued for unused time (per our Terms of Service)
  • For Founder Deal members: Your lifetime access will be terminated
