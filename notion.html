FAQ

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Do I need Notion?

Yes. This version is built specifically for Notion users who want voice task capture connected to a Notion database.

Do I need Blurts Pro?

The launch bundle includes 3 months of Blurts Pro so you can fully test the voice-to-Notion workflow.

Can I use my own Notion database?

Yes. You can use Blurts with your existing task database, or start with the included Command Center template.

Can I customize the categories?

Yes. You can rename areas, priorities, statuses, and views inside Notion. The template is meant to be a starting system, not a locked setup.

Does this work on iOS and Android?

Yes. Blurts is available on both iOS and Android.

What happens after 3 months of Blurts Pro?

You can continue with the free version of Blurts or upgrade to a paid Blurts plan.

Is this a full life planner?

No. It is intentionally focused on voice task capture, errands, follow-ups, and weekly task organization. It is not a giant second brain template.
